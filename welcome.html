❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does this work on YouTube Kids? ▼
Yes! The extension fully supports both YouTube and YouTube Kids. It automatically detects which platform you're using and adapts accordingly.
Why was this extension created? ▼
This extension was born from a real need - watching my nephew accidentally press keys while watching videos. It's designed specifically for parents and families who want uninterrupted viewing experiences for their children.
Can I customize which keys are blocked? ▼
Yes! Click the extension icon and you can choose which keys remain active in fullscreen. Escape key always works to ensure you can exit fullscreen.
Does this collect any data? ▼
No. We respect your privacy completely. The extension works entirely offline, doesn't collect any data, and doesn't track your viewing habits.
Will this slow down my browser? ▼
Not at all! The extension is extremely lightweight and only activates when you're in fullscreen mode on YouTube. It has virtually no impact on browser performance.
